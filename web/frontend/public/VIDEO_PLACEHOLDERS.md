# Video Background Placeholders

Place your video files here:
- `/public/LS.mp4` - Light theme background video
- `/public/DS.mp4` - Dark theme background video

Recommended specs:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- Duration: 10-30 seconds (looped)
- Format: H.264 MP4
- File size: < 20MB for fast loading
