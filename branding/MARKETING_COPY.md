## Brand Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Lead with business outcomes, not technical features
- Use active, confident language
- Emphasize simplicity and speed
- Include concrete metrics where possible
- Speak to both technical and business audiences

### Don't
- Overuse AI buzzwords and hype
- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Focus solely on technical implementation
- Use fear-based messaging
- Sound generic or corporate-speak
